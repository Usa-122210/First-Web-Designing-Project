Travel Essentials to Pack

  • Passport, visa documents, and travel insurance papers – keep photocopies separately.

  • Comfortable clothing and weather-appropriate footwear – layering helps adapt to climates.

  • Power bank, universal chargers, and adapters – essential for staying connected.

  • Reusable water bottle and eco-friendly toiletries – reduce plastic waste.

  • Emergency medications and a small first-aid kit – better safe than sorry.

  • Camera or smartphone with extra storage for photos – capture your memories.

Travel Terms

Itinerary
A day-by-day outline of your travel schedule including transfers, meals, and activities.
Visa
Official permission from a country allowing you to enter and stay for a specific period.
Layover
The waiting time at an intermediate airport between connecting flights.
